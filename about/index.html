Our Story

Founded in 1984, Lexington Machine Corp has established itself as a leader in precision manufacturing. Our journey began with a single manual mill and a commitment to absolute accuracy. Over the decades, we have evolved into a high-end facility housing state-of-the-art CNC technology.

Our dedication to quality and complex engineering solutions has made us a trusted partner for aerospace, medical, and defense industries worldwide. We don't just manufacture parts; we engineer reliability into every component that leaves our floor.

Today, with over 100,000 square feet of production space, we continue to push the boundaries of what is possible in metalworking, maintaining the same artisan attention to detail that defined our company on day one.
